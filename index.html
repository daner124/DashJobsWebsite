About the App

book Inspiration

Want to make money but on your own schedule? Tired of scouring the internet or going door to door to find side jobs? Look no further than Dash Jobs! With its simplistic design users can easily scroll through a map of their city and find pins on side jobs, go and complete them, and get paid! Seriously, it's that simple...

shopping_bag Pricing

Dash jobs is a completely FREE app that takes away the difficulty of finding side jobs and does this through an elegant yet feature-packed interface. The app is easy to use for beginners yet provides experienced users with plenty of information and tools that they desire. But at the end of the day it's FREE so why not try it out!

touch_app Features

There will be many features that make working your side job exciting rather than a grind. Features will include points upon job completions that can be redeemed, a global and a friends only scoreboard to encourage friendly competition, special events from sponsors that will give users great rewards, and so much more!
